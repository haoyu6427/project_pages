3D-URRT: Unsupervised Region-Aware Registration Transformer

Yu Hao1, Yi Fang1,2*
1NYU Multimedia and Visual Computing Lab, 2NYUAD Center for AI & Robotics
*Corresponding Author
ICIP 2024
Paper

Overview

Comparison between the shape-conditioned transformation and region-conditioned transformation.

Abstract

This paper concerns the research problem of point cloud registration to find the rigid transformation to optimally align the source point set with the target one. Existing methods rely on global shape descriptors and shape-conditioned decoders. In contrast, we propose a region-aware method, called 3D-URRT, which leverages a 3D region partition module, a 3D shape transformer, and a region-aware decoder to predict region-specific transformations. The global transformation is computed by weighted fusion of region-based outputs. Trained with self-supervised 3D reconstruction and alignment losses, 3D-URRT outperforms state-of-the-art methods on various benchmarks such as ModelNet40.

Method

Our proposed 3D-URRT framework contains three main components: 3D region partition module, 3D shape transformer module and region-aware decoder module.
